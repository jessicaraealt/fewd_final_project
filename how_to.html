How do I reserve a cart?

Click on a cart to access its calendar, reservation form, and information about the devices and location of each cart. To reserve, select the start and end time for your reservations. This allows teachers to reserve carts for specific times in the day, so that the cart can be used by multiple people in one day.

How to I get a cart after I reserve it?

You should have at least one student in your class who is a member of the Mouse Squad. If not, identify a student that you trust and send them to pick up the laptop cart at the beginning of your reserved time. If you try to pick up the cart early, it might be reserved to another teacher. Remember to tell the student the room number where the cart lives. The cart should be returned to its home room at the end of class.

What are teachers responsible for?

  • You are responsible for reserving carts each and every time you use them. This helps us collect data about who uses the carts and how often they are in use.
  • You are responsible for checking the contents of the cart at the start of your reservation, making sure that all devices are present and that the laptop cart is organized.
  • You are responsible for ensuring that all devices are returned to the cart and stored appropriately, meaning they are in the correct order and are plugged in to charge.
  • You are responsible for keeping the cart plugged in and charging while it is in your classroom.
